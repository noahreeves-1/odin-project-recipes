Japanese Curry

Description

This Japanese curry recipe is a classic dish that is served in homes and in restaurants around the world.

Ingredients

  • white rice
  • curry roux
  • potatoes
  • carrots
  • onions
  • beef chuck or stew meat

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice and begin cooking
  2. chop up onions, potatoes, and carrots
  3. chop up beef into smaller chunks
  4. heat up a pot with medium heat
  5. once hot, add some oil and the chopped onions
  6. once onions have caramelized and are slightly yellow, take them out
  7. in the same pot, sear the meat on all sides. Make sure not to cook for too long as it could over-cook the beef.
  8. Once the beef is cooked, add the cut potatoes and carrots, and add water until all of the ingredients are submerged.
  9. Let the stew boil for 20 minutes then add the curry roux and stir well until all roux as been dissolved and evenly mixed with the stew.
  10. Grab a dish, serve some rice, pour your curry in, and voila! Dinner is served!
