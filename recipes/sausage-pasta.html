Sausage Pasta

Description

This cheesy pasta is guaranteed to impress any dinner guests you may have. There are a number of ways to make this dish, but we highly recommend this recipe as it has been a success for us time and time again.

Ingredients

  • pasta
  • sausage
  • marinara sauce
  • parmesan asiago
  • cheddar cheese
  • salt
  • pepper
  • red pepper
  • parsley

Steps

  1. boil water
  2. add pasta and salt to boiling water
  3. stir for a few minutes to prevent pasta from sticking
  4. cook sausage on frying pan to your satisfaction and set aside once done
  5. once pasta is done, drain the water and add the cooked sausage and marinara sauce to the pan/pot
  6. add red pepper flakes, cheese, and pepper and stir while keeping the heat on medium-low
